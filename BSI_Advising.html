BSI Academic Advising

Welcome to the BSI Academic Advising resource site. Here, you'll find information to help you plan your academic journey, meet degree requirements, and access essential student support services.

Note: This site is designed for active BSI students. Make sure you are logged into your UMICH account to view restricted resources.

Academic Resources

The University of Michigan School of Information (UMSI) provides a range of academic resources to support students throughout their academic journey:

Academic Advising

Collaborate with an academic advisor to develop individualized plans, interpret policies, and navigate challenges. Review preparation tips before your session to make the most of your time.

Degree Planning

Understand BSI degree requirements and track your progress. Cohort-specific requirements ensure your coursework aligns with the program structure.

Registration & Policies

Find guidance on course registration, policies, and accessing relevant forms. Use resources like WolverineAccess for easy management of your academic progress.

Student Success & Support

UMSI offers extensive support to ensure student success both academically and personally:

Academic Success and Tutoring

Access free programming and math tutoring or connect with the Academic Success Team for skill-building, accommodations, or navigating challenges.

Student Support Resources

Utilize resources for health, wellness, community building, and emergency support. Whether you need counseling, food assistance, or campus connections, help is available.
